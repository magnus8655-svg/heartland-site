## Selected Approach: Neo-Brutalism with Soft Touch

This approach best serves the psychological objectives because:

1. **Authenticity signals trust:** The unpolished, honest aesthetic directly counters the "slick salesperson" fear
2. **Asymmetry creates engagement:** Breaking grid patterns keeps attention without aggressive tactics
3. **Tactile elements feel real:** Thick borders and card-based layouts make digital feel tangible and trustworthy
4. **Minimal animation respects attention:** No tricks or distractions—just clear, purposeful motion

The design will feel like a straightforward conversation with a competent neighbor, not a corporate pitch.
